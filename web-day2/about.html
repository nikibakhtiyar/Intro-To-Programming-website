About Me

Hi, I'm Niki Bakhtiyar, also known as Niqi. I'm a techno DJ and producer based in Boston, currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music.

I focus on hypnotic, percussive, stripped-down techno with a cinematic edge. I perform using hardware, custom sound design, and live improvisation.

When I'm not producing or DJing, I'm exploring new ways to merge sound design, technology, and storytelling.
